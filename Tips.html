This video must have given you a clear picture on how to protect yourself.

5 Things to follow, in order to protect yourself from cyberattacks

  1. Limit the personal information you share online
  2. Change privacy settings and do not use location features
  3. Keep software applications and operating systems up-to-date.
  4. Create strong passwords by using upper and lower case letters, numbers and special characters
  5. Think twice before clicking on links or opening attachments
